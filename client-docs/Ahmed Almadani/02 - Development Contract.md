## 1. Project Description

The Developer agrees to design, develop, and deliver a **KSA Furniture Marketplace Platform** (the "Project"), comprising:

- A backend API platform providing all services described in [[03 - Scope of Work]]
- A mobile application for iOS and Android platforms
- Deployment support and technical handoff

The complete scope is defined in the attached [[03 - Scope of Work]] and [[01 - Invoice #MKT-2026-001]], both of which are incorporated herein by reference.

---

## 2. Compensation and Payment Terms

### 2.1 Total Compensation
The Client agrees to pay the Developer a total fixed fee of **$16,400 USD** (Sixteen Thousand Four Hundred United States Dollars) for all deliverables described in this Agreement.

### 2.2 Payment Schedule
> [!important] Payment is due **100% upon project delivery and acceptance**.

| Event | Amount Due |
|-------|-----------|
| Project Completion & Client Acceptance | $16,400 USD (100%) |
| **Total** | **$16,400 USD** |

**No advance payment, deposit, or milestone payment is required.** The full amount becomes due only after:
1. The Developer notifies the Client that all deliverables are complete.
2. The Client reviews the delivered work.
3. The Client provides written acceptance (email confirmation is sufficient).

### 2.3 Payment Method
Payment shall be made via wire transfer, PayPal, or another method mutually agreed upon in writing before project kickoff.

### 2.4 Late Payment
If payment is not received within **14 calendar days** of client acceptance, a late fee of **1.5% per month** on the outstanding balance shall accrue.

---

## 3. Timeline and Delivery

### 3.1 Estimated Duration
The estimated project duration is **10 to 12 weeks** from the date both Parties sign this Agreement ("Project Kickoff").

### 3.2 Timeline Adjustments
The Developer shall notify the Client promptly of any circumstances that may affect the timeline. Extensions shall be agreed upon in writing. Delays caused by the Client (e.g., failure to provide required content, accounts, or feedback) shall not be counted against the Developer's timeline.

### 3.3 Delivery
Delivery consists of:
- A working production-ready backend API and mobile application
- Deployment to an agreed-upon environment
- Access credentials and technical handoff documentation

---

## 4. Revisions and Change Requests

### 4.1 Included Revisions
During development, the Developer will accommodate reasonable revision requests that fall within the scope defined in [[03 - Scope of Work]].

### 4.2 Out-of-Scope Requests
Any features, modifications, or additions outside the agreed scope will be quoted separately and require a written amendment to this Agreement before work begins.

### 4.3 Change Order Process
The Client must submit change requests in writing. The Developer will respond with a quote within 5 business days.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Client Ownership Upon Payment
Upon receipt of **full payment**, the Developer assigns to the Client all rights, title, and interest in the final delivered software, including all source code and design assets created specifically for this Project.

### 5.2 Developer Retains Pre-Existing IP
The Developer retains all rights to:
- Frameworks, libraries, tools, and methodologies used in the development
- Reusable code components not created exclusively for this Project
- Any proprietary development processes, architectures, or patterns

### 5.3 No Transfer Before Payment
**No intellectual property rights transfer to the Client until full payment has been received and cleared.**

### 5.4 Portfolio Rights
The Developer reserves the right to reference this Project in their portfolio, case studies, or professional profiles, using only publicly visible and non-confidential information.

---

## 6. Confidentiality

### 6.1 Client Confidentiality
The Developer agrees to keep all business information, user data, and non-public details provided by the Client strictly confidential and will not disclose them to third parties.

### 6.2 Developer Confidentiality
The Client agrees to keep all technical implementations, system designs, software architecture, proprietary methods, and processes delivered by the Developer confidential. The Client shall not:
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, replicate, or repurpose the Developer's underlying technical approach
- Share the technical implementation details with any third party without the Developer's written consent
- Hire third parties to replicate or extend the system based on confidential technical information obtained through this project

### 6.3 Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of **3 years**.

---

## 7. Independent Contractor

The Developer is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, or joint venture relationship. The Developer is solely responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and professional expenses.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

The Client is solely responsible for the costs and accounts of third-party services required to operate the platform, including but not limited to:
- Cloud hosting and servers
- Payment gateway accounts and transaction fees
- SMS and email delivery services
- Cloud storage and media hosting
- App Store and Google Play developer accounts

The Developer will integrate with these services but is not responsible for their ongoing costs or terms.

---

## 9. Warranties and Limitation of Liability

### 9.1 Developer Warranty
The Developer warrants that:
- The delivered software will function as described in [[03 - Scope of Work]]
- The software is original work and does not knowingly infringe any third-party intellectual property
- The Developer will address material defects discovered within **30 days** of delivery at no additional cost

### 9.2 Limitation of Liability
In no event shall either Party be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages. The Developer's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client.

---

## 10. Termination

### 10.1 Termination by Client
The Client may terminate this Agreement with **14 days written notice**. If the Client terminates after development work has begun, the Client shall pay for all work completed to the termination date at a pro-rated rate.

### 10.2 Termination by Developer
The Developer may terminate this Agreement if the Client materially breaches any term and fails to remedy the breach within 10 business days of written notice.

### 10.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination, each Party shall return or destroy the other Party's confidential information. No intellectual property rights transfer on termination without full payment.
